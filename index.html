Don't know your latitude and longitude?

  • In the United States, use the Geographical Name Server at the U.S. Bureau of the Census.
  • In England, Wales and Scotland, convert Ordnance Survey grid coordinates to latitude and longitude.
  • In Northern Ireland, convert Irish Grid coordinates to latitude and longitude.

  • A trio of Java applets to convert between OSGB, OSNI and latitude/longitude.
    • OSGB (British National Grid) to latitude/longitude
    • OSNI (Irish Grid) to latitude/longitude
    • OSNI (Irish Grid) to OSGB (British National Grid)
